WEIGHTLIFTING

weightlifting to develop physical strength and/or a muscular physique. It is a common part of strength conditioning for athletes in many sports. When the primary goal is to develop an all-round muscular physique, this is bodybuilding.

CROSSFIT

CrossFit is a group exercise program that combines elements of cardiovascular conditioning, strength training and functional training. CrossFit is a full-body exercise program that uses a wide variety of equipment, including a barbell, kettlebells, dumbbells, medicine balls, and body weight.

BOXING

Boxing is a combat sport in which two people, usually wearing protective gloves, throw punches at each other for a predetermined set of time in a boxing ring. The winner is the person who scores the most points by landing the punches first.

YOGA

Yoga is a physical, mental, and spiritual practice that originated in India. It has many physical and mental benefits, including improved flexibility, strength, and balance, as well as reduced stress and anxiety.
