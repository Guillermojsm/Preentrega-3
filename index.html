BENEFITS OF EXERCISING

Exercising has so many benefits that can truly improve your overall well-being. For starters, it’s great for your heart and circulation, helping to lower blood pressure and reduce the risk of cardiovascular problems. Plus, physical activity releases endorphins, which can really boost your mood, reducing stress, anxiety, and even symptoms of depression. You also get the bonus of better sleep, since regular movement helps you fall asleep faster and enjoy more restful nights.

NUTRITION ADVICE

Proper nutrition is key for muscle growth and recovery. Focus on a balanced intake of macronutrients:

  • Protein: Essential for muscle repair and growth. Aim for lean sources like chicken, fish, and eggs.
  • Carbohydrates:Provide energy for workouts. Opt for complex carbs like rice, oats, and potatoes.
  • Fats: Support hormone production. Healthy fats include avocados, nuts, and olive oil.

Stay hydrated! Drinking at least 3 liters of water per day is crucial.

Quick Meal Plan

  • Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with whole wheat toast and avocado.
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken with brown rice and steamed vegetables.
  • Dinner: Salmon with quinoa and a side of salad.
